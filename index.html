Best armors in Fallout 4



Power Armor Quantum X-01 Power armor

The Quantum X-01 power armor is identical in stats and model to standard X-01 power armor but bears a unique custom paint job in a variety of shades of blue in the theme of the new Nuka-Cola Quantum flavor. It also displays the Nuka-Cola Quantum logo embossed in metal, automotive-style lettering on the left breastplate. Read more

Nuka-Girl rocketsuit

A recreation of the costume worn by the corporate mascot, Nuka-Girl. Equipping it provides +5 Damage Resistance, +45 Energy Resistance and +35 Radiation Resistance, as well as +1 to Charisma and the ability to breathe underwater, similar to rank 1 of Aquaboy Aquagirl. Because it covers the entire body, nothing else may be worn with the armor. It also cannot be upgraded with ballistic weave. Read more

The Silver Shroud

A recreation of the costume worn by the comic book character, the Silver Shroud. Equipping both parts grants a temporary 15% reduction to damage dealt by human and ghoul enemies and temporary +1 to Agility with the coat as well as a temporary +1 to Perception with the hat. Read more

Raider Power armor

It is used by some raider leaders and high-level raiders. Raider power armor has the worst statistics of any type of power armor and has few upgrade options. A full set of raider power armor provides 500 points of Damage Resistance and 250 points of Energy Resistance. It can be cheaply upgraded to Raider II armor to increase the DR to 620 points. In comparison with the first two versions of T-45 armor, raider power armor has the same damage resistance but worse energy resistance. Raider power armor can be repaired with steel. This armor does not spawn by level. Read more
